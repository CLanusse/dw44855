Arvo Pärt won the Spanish High Prize

The Spanish BBVA Foundation today announced the winner of the Frontiers of Knowledge music field, the prize went to composer Arvo Pärt. The award is presented in eight categories and recognizes outstanding achievements in the fields of science and technology, humanities and music. The name of the award suggests that the laureates have managed to shift the boundaries of our knowledge from the world.

Ten years since the premiere of Adam's Lament

The premiere of Arvo Pärt's work "Adam's Lament" took place in cooperation with the European Capitals of Culture Istanbul 2010 and Tallinn 2011, which commissioned the work, on June 7, 2010 in the Hagia Irene Church-Concert Hall in Istanbul. Mikko Fritze, the director of the Tallinn 2011 Foundation, looks back at this event.

The exhibition of the Arvo Pärt Center is like a journey to become an artist

On July 1, the Arvo Pärt Center will open an exhibition entitled "If You Are Searching from Scratch", which for the first time will present the thoughts recorded in Arvo Pärt's diaries to a wider audience. With its first permanent exhibition, the center continues a series of events dedicated to Arvo Pärt's 85th birthday.
